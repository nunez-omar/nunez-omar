Greenium Project

This research paper explores the presence of a "greenium" in green bonds relative to conventional bonds before and after the enactment of The Inflation Reduction Act. Employing a K-nearest-neighbor algorithm with Mahalanobis distance, the study aims to to match green bonds with conventional counterparts, thereby evaluating the impact of the IRA on green bond pricing.

Principal Component Analysis

Group project focuses on employing Principal Component Analysis (PCA) to fixed income securities to develop robust trading and hedging strategies. By decomposing the yield curve into its principal components, the project aims to identify underlying factors driving interest rate movements. The results will be used to construct effective strategies that mitigate risk and enhance portfolio performance in the fixed income market.

Entrprenuership

Establishment of a start-up honey production company in the Dominican Republic, targeting the exportation and distribution of premium honey to the U.S. consumer market. Leveraging the rich biodiversity and favorable climate of the Dominican Republic, the company aims to produce high-quality, organic honey. The strategic focus is on sustainable practices, fair trade partnerships, and meeting the growing demand for natural and ethically sourced honey in the United States.
